Short bio

I was born in Kerala, India. I am currently working as a Research Associate in the Computer Graphics and Visualization Group at TU Delft. Previously, I was an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT Guwahati), India (2020) and a Postdoctoral Researcher (2019) in the GeoVic (formerly STREAM) team of LIX, Ecole Polytechnique, Paris, working with Marie-Paule Cani . Before that, I was a Ph.D. student and then an Institute Pre-Doctoral Fellow in the Advanced Geometric Computing Lab (AGCL) at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, India, working with Ramanathan Muthuganapathy. During my Ph.D, I visited University of Tokyo and worked with Takeo Igarashi.

I am interested in the practical algorithms for digital content creation tasks with the main focus on Sketch-based interfaces. I also work on fundamental problems in digital geometry processing, including reconstruction and meshing.
